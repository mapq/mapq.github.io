¡Bienvenidos!

Welcome! I am a professor of computer science/computing. My current position is Full Professor in Software and Information Systems in the College of Computing and Informatics at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte.

I will be receiving the 2025 ACM SIGCSE Distinguished Service to Computer Science Education in Pittsburgh, PA!

Research

My research is in Human-computer interaction and Computer Science Education. Some areas that I have studied include personal information management, information refinding, tools for building interactive systems, digital libraries, use of computers for education, and cultural influence in interface design.

Teaching

Currently I teach and I'm the course coordinator of ITSC 2214 Data Structures and Algorithms.

Professional Service

I participate on a number of groups and organizations inside and outside of UNCC as part of my commitment to Diversity, Equity and Inclusion in academia and professional service.

  • Deputy Chair of the ACM Distinguished Service Award committee.
  • Member of the Board of Directors for the Computing Research Association, and co-chair of the CRA Leadership Academy.
  • Member of the Committee on Women in Science, Engineering and Medicine at the National Academies of Science, Engineering.
  • Co-Founder of the Hispanics in Computing group and manager of their listserv and website.
  • Website manager for the SIGCSE organization.
