Overview

Discussion of diversity issues as represented in short stories (literature!). The stories allow us to explore topics in a "fictional" setting with clear connections to society.

Learning Objectives

To be provided:

  1. Discuss the importance of representation in literature
  2. Understand issues of social justice from different lenses
  3. Discuss racial identity/racism as represented in the stories
