Overview

The goal of this module is to explore the topic of Race and its definition and how it has been used to implement and support racism in the US. This is a difficult topic for some, challenging for others. I have provided some readings, but there are a lot of other readings in the Additional Resources tab that if you are interested in learning more, I strongly encourage you to explore. Because Diversity & Inclusion is tightly intertwined with race and racism, there are other books and readings listed through out the additional resources sections in this course.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

  1. Understand the definition and history of race
  2. Understand Systemic Racism
  3. Describe examples of racism in everyday life
