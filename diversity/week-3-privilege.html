Overview

The goal of this section is to give you a deeper understanding of the notion of privilege. Privilege sometimes is seen or used as an attack or as an insult. Privilege often is something you have because society gives it to you, not because anything you have done to earn it. Privilege is at the root of inequities in society.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

  1. Define and understand privilege
  2. Identify examples in society of how privilege opens and closes doors for groups of people
